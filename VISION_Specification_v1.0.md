VISION.md Specification v1.0

Purpose

Define the project's long-term purpose, enduring beliefs, and desired future.

Questions It Must Answer

Why should this project exist?
What future do we believe in?
Which beliefs are non-negotiable?
What do we explicitly reject?
What future are we trying to create?
What is our North Star?

Must NOT Contain

Features
Requirements
Roadmaps
Technologies
Architecture
APIs
Implementation details
Sprint plans

Writing Principles

Technology-independent
Valid for at least ten years
Stable over the project lifetime
Focus on beliefs instead of plans
Inspire decisions instead of describing execution
